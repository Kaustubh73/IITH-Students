The 'General' Council

The 'General' Council is an umbrella council that is supposed to look after all the other councils and also take care of the student's welfare by providing them with a zoo in the name of hostel :). You can find everyone here :D The General Council is leaded by two General Secretaries.One shall be girl student and another a boy.Mess Secretaries,Transport Heads and Hostel Representatives constitute the rest of the Council.The General council strives towards general welfare of the students.
